Our Services

Classes

Our makerspace offers diverse classes where members can learn woodworking techniques, 3D printing and design, electronics prototyping, laser cutting, sewing and textiles, coding and programming, digital design and fabrication, and business and entrepreneurship. These classes provide hands-on learning experiences to acquire practical skills and knowledge, empowering members to pursue their creative projects within our vibrant makerspace community.

Expert Guidance

Our makerspace offers Expert Guidance, connecting members with experienced staff and mentors who provide personalized assistance, advice, and troubleshooting support. Our experts have diverse expertise in woodworking, electronics, 3D printing, and more, helping you enhance your skills and achieve your creative goals with confidence.
